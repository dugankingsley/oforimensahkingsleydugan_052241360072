THE VISION AND OBJECTIVES OF THE DEPARTMENTS

All the departments in the faculty of applied science and technology offers PHD programs,master's programs and undergraduates programs exposing students to a broad range of methods using tools from mathematical ,computational sciences etc. Developing skills and expertise in problem articulation and solving, and abilities to appreciate and accommodate uncertainty in decision-making,.The Departments continuously engages in significant collaborations with each other and units at Duke, at other universities, in government, and in industry. We wholeheartedly embrace interdisciplinary research and education; they are part of the fabric of our mission.
